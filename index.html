Hi i'm Benjamin Hughes

I am a lecturer currently working at kea.dk teaching software development. I'm into Generative AI, teaching computer science, and creating weird/quirky projects.

Previously i have been an Educational Director at HackYourFuture, consultant at It-minds, developed interactive news graphics at Danmarks Radio, been a co-founder in a startup called Sunmapper and developed user friendly interfaces for a muslim dating app.

These days i'm doing two research projects one is about how software developers use Generative AI. The other is about using LLM's to simulate real people for answering surveys questions.
